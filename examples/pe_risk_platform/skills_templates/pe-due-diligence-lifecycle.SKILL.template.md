# PE Due Diligence Lifecycle Skill

Use this lifecycle to produce a realistic investment committee quality risk packet.

## Stage 1: Thesis and market validation
- Validate why this asset is winnable in the sponsor's ownership window.
- Stress-test growth assumptions versus cycle and pricing-power evidence.

## Stage 2: Commercial diligence
- Revenue durability: concentration, churn, NRR, renewal profile.
- Channel and cohort risk: identify hidden volume cliffs.

## Stage 3: Financial diligence (QoE + cash)
- Separate normalized EBITDA from one-off adjustments.
- Evaluate cash conversion and working-capital volatility.
- Re-underwrite debt service under downside scenario.

## Stage 4: Legal and regulatory diligence
- Inventory litigation, compliance, and regulatory triggers.
- Elevate any unresolved critical/high flags to IC-level blockers.

## Stage 5: Value creation and execution readiness
- Test whether value creation depends on multiple perfect outcomes.
- Validate management bench strength and integration capacity.

## Stage 6: IC recommendation framing
Your response must include exactly these sections:

1. Risk Register
2. Open Diligence Questions
3. Go / No-go Recommendation

Use concise, decision-grade language with explicit conditions precedent where needed.
